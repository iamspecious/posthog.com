<details>
<summary><strong>Goal: Create pipeline automation across open roles</strong></summary>

**Drivers:** Coua, Ellie

**Motivation:** We have several evergreen and highly specialized roles to fill. Building pipeline automation will create an additional source of qualified candidates, strengthen the top of the funnel, and help us achieve our hiring goals.

### We'll know we're successful when:

- We've successfully built AI skills and automations for our evergreen roles and selected high-priority, specialized roles.
- We begin generating qualified candidates through these automations, and those candidates convert into the interview process.
- The process becomes repeatable and scalable for future roles.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Goal: Increase inbound and outbound sourcing efforts</strong></summary>

**Driver:** Zbynek

**Motivation:** Increasing both inbound and outbound sourcing efforts will strengthen the top of the funnel, resulting in higher-quality candidates and greater talent density.

### We'll know we're successful when:

- We've created a metrics-driven hiring model that shows how many interviews are needed to achieve a target number of hires.
- We've successfully used that model to determine the right balance between inbound and outbound sourcing needed to meet hiring goals.
- We've run a one-month experiment with paid advertising to evaluate its impact on candidate quality and hiring outcomes.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Goal: All team members are proficient in AI and can build their own automations</strong></summary>

**Drivers:** Coua, Ellie, Julia, Mark, Rune, Zbynek

**Motivation:** Building automations will increase our productivity, reduce time spent on manual administrative work, and allow us to spend more time sourcing and engaging high-quality candidates.

### We'll know we're successful when:

- Every single team member has built something to automate a manual workflow (i.e. referral reminder, announcement on open roles, anything repetitive in our work) or a tooling to improve help us be more efficient
- We regularly share learnings and demo the AI tools and automations we've built with one another.
- Team members are successfully using one another's tools and workflows.
- Every member of the Talent team feels confident using AI, regardless of the complexity of the tooling they've have built.

</details>
