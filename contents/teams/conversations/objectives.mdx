## Q3 2026 Objectives

### Objective 1: Launch Support to general availability
- Product is officially GA
- 100% of PostHog support tickets flow through Conversations
- Zendesk is stopped

### Objective 2: AI bot resolves tickets (private beta)
- AI bot available in private beta for a group of customers (PostHog is first)
- Satisfaction tracking in place (thumbs up/down + notes)
- 90% satisfaction on AI-resolved tickets

### Objective 3: Support AI bot feeds Tasks
- AI bot detects when a ticket needs a bug fix, docs update, or similar change
- Support becomes a source for Tasks, which creates PRs from there

### Objective 4: Business knowledge becomes a hive mind
- Every knowledge item links to related items — connections between rows, not just search hits
- Agents traverse those connections to pull the bigger picture, not only fetch a single answer

### Objective 5: Hire a new team member
- Scope of work across GA, AI bot, Tasks, and knowledge is large for the current team of one
- Hire and onboard a new engineer in Q3
